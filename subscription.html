# Subscription Terms _Last updated: 26 January, 2026_ FreeNic offers a paid subscription that unlocks additional features and extended insights. ### Payment and renewal - Subscriptions are billed through your Google Play account. - Payment is charged automatically at the beginning of each billing period. - Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. ### Cancellation - You can cancel your subscription at any time through Google Play subscription settings. - After cancellation, the subscription remains active until the end of the already paid period. - No refunds are provided for unused time unless required by applicable law. ### Price changes - If the subscription price changes, you will be notified in advance via Google Play. - The new price will only apply after your confirmation if required by Google Play policies. ### Free trial (if available) - If a free trial is offered, payment will be charged only after the trial period ends. - Free trials are available once per Google Play account unless stated otherwise. ### Restore purchases - If your subscription is not detected, you can use the “Restore purchases” option. - Active subscriptions linked to your Google Play account will be restored. ### Important - Subscriptions are linked to your Google Play account and cannot be transferred. - Deleting the app does not cancel your subscription.
